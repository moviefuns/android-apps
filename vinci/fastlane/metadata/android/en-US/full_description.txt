Vinci is a personal CRM for Android that syncs everything to your own Nextcloud. Stay on top of your relationships without giving your data to anyone else.

LOG INTERACTIONS
Keep a record of every call, meeting, email, message, or social media interaction. Add type, reason, notes, outcome, and tags. Set a follow-up reminder that syncs to Tasks.org.

STAY ON TOP OF PEOPLE
The home screen shows starred contacts, recent interactions, and upcoming birthdays at a glance. Never forget who you were supposed to call back.

CONTACT DETAILS
See all contact info alongside your full interaction history. Tap to call, message, email, or open WhatsApp and Signal directly from the contact screen.

YOUR DATA, YOUR NEXTCLOUD
All interaction logs, follow-ups, and attachments are stored on your own Nextcloud via WebDAV. Contacts are read from DAVx5. Vinci never connects to any server other than yours.

PRIVACY FIRST
No analytics. No crash reporting. No ads. No tracking of any kind.

FEATURES
- Interaction log with type, reason, notes, outcome, and tags
- Social Media interaction type with platform picker (LinkedIn, Instagram, GitHub, and more)
- Starred contacts for quick access
- Follow-up reminders via Tasks.org / CalDAV
- Birthday reminders on the home screen
- Tag filtering on the Contacts tab
- Bulk star/unstar via long-press
- Per-contact Markdown export
- File attachments stored on Nextcloud
- WhatsApp and Signal deep links
- Text size setting (Small / Default / Large / Extra Large)
- Biometric app lock
- Full dark mode support
- Nextcloud Login Flow v2

REQUIREMENTS
- Nextcloud instance (self-hosted)
- DAVx5 or compatible CardDAV app
- Tasks.org (optional, for follow-up reminders)
